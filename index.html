About Me

Hello, and welcome to my personal website!

My name is Rahul Chari, and I'm a fourth-year student at the University of Florida. I'm pursuing a major in computer science and a minor in linguistics. I created this website to showcase my projects, skills, and interests. See if you can find all my easter eggs!

I first developed an interest in programming after taking a Java course in high school. I immediately took a liking to it, and decided I would major in computer science in college. During my time at UF, I've successfully completed two internships and one term studying abroad at Kyoto University. I currently serve as a tutor for the UF CISE Department and a TA for the Operating Systems course, where I seek to empower others through teaching.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling (my favorite destinations are Japan and Korea!), hiking, weightlifting, bouldering, and playing basketball. I also like creating fun programming projects that combine my personal interests and needs.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at any time!
